Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

PRIVACY-FIRST APPROACH: Aetheron is a decentralized platform. We do not collect personal information or require account registration. Your wallet interactions are pseudonymous and recorded on the public blockchain.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Aetheron Platform ("we," "us," or "Platform") handles information when you use our decentralized finance services. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing transparent information about data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

As a decentralized platform, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account registration data
  • KYC (Know Your Customer) information
  • Private keys or wallet passwords
  • Financial information beyond public blockchain data

3. Information That Is Public

When you interact with Aetheron smart contracts, the following information is recorded on the Polygon blockchain and is publicly accessible:

  • Your wallet address
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps
  • Staking positions and rewards
  • Smart contract interactions
  • Token balances

Note: Blockchain data is permanent and cannot be deleted.

4. Technical Information

When you visit our website, we may collect standard technical information:

Data Type Purpose Duration
IP Address Security, analytics Not stored
Browser Type Compatibility Session only
Device Information Responsive design Session only
Page Views Usage statistics Aggregated only

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and browser local storage for:

  • Service Worker: Caching for offline functionality
  • Theme Preferences: Storing your dark/light mode choice
  • Wallet Connection: Remembering your last connected wallet (locally only)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. The Platform will still function, but some features may be limited.

6. Third-Party Services

The Platform integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

6.1 Wallet Providers (MetaMask, etc.)

When you connect your wallet, you communicate directly with the wallet provider. Review their privacy policy:

  • MetaMask: metamask.io/privacy.html

6.2 Blockchain Network (Polygon)

All transactions are recorded on the Polygon blockchain. Polygon nodes may collect technical data. Review Polygon's privacy policy.

6.3 Decentralized Exchanges (QuickSwap)

When you trade on QuickSwap, their privacy policy applies. All trades are visible on the blockchain.

6.4 Price Data Providers

We may display price data from third-party APIs. These providers may collect usage data.

7. How We Use Information

The minimal technical information we collect is used to:

  • Ensure Platform security and prevent attacks
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • Generate aggregated usage statistics
  • Comply with legal obligations (if required)

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your information with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Security: To investigate fraud or security issues
  • Service Providers: Only if necessary for Platform operation (e.g., hosting)

9. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for website traffic
  • No storage of private keys or passwords
  • Regular security audits (when possible)
  • Minimal data collection principle

However: No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for protecting your wallet and private keys.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

GDPR (European Users)

  • Right to access data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (limited for blockchain data)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

CCPA (California Users)

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

Note: Blockchain data cannot be deleted or modified due to the nature of distributed ledger technology.

11. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the Platform, please contact us.

12. International Data Transfers

The Platform is hosted on the internet and blockchain networks, which are global. Your data may be processed in various jurisdictions. By using the Platform, you consent to international data transfers.

13. Data Retention

We retain minimal technical data for:

  • Session data: Until you close your browser
  • Local storage: Until you clear browser data
  • Blockchain data: Permanent (cannot be deleted)

14. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics services to understand Platform usage. These services:

  • Do not track you across websites
  • Do not use invasive cookies
  • Aggregate data anonymously

15. Do Not Track

We respect "Do Not Track" signals. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will not track your activity beyond essential Platform functionality.

16. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

17. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to external websites (DEXs, block explorers, etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before sharing data.

Abstract

Aetheron (AETH) is a decentralized finance ecosystem built on the Polygon blockchain, designed to provide users with accessible, high-yield staking opportunities, social trading features, and comprehensive portfolio management tools. By leveraging Polygon's Layer 2 scaling solution, Aetheron delivers near-instantaneous transactions with minimal fees while maintaining Ethereum's security guarantees.

This litepaper outlines the technical architecture, tokenomics, staking mechanics, governance structure, payment integrations, and roadmap for the Aetheron platform.

Payment Integrations

Aetheron supports multiple crypto payment gateways for user convenience and global access. The following payment providers are integrated:

  • Coinbase Commerce: Accepts BTC, ETH, USDC, and more. Simple API, no KYC for basic use, and funds go directly to your wallet.
  • BitPay: Supports Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, and several stablecoins. Offers merchant tools, settlement in crypto or fiat, and robust APIs.
  • NOWPayments: Accepts 100+ cryptocurrencies, offers auto-conversion to your preferred coin, and has a straightforward API for integration.

These solutions allow users to participate using a wide range of cryptocurrencies, ensuring flexibility and accessibility for a global audience.

1. Introduction

1.1 The Problem

The DeFi landscape faces several critical challenges:

  • High Transaction Costs: Ethereum gas fees often exceed $50-100 per transaction, making DeFi inaccessible to small-capital users
  • Complex User Experience: Fragmented protocols require multiple platforms for staking, trading, and portfolio management
  • Limited Earning Opportunities: Traditional staking yields are often below 5% APY
  • Lack of Social Features: DeFi protocols rarely incorporate community-driven trading and learning
  • Poor Governance: Many projects have centralized decision-making or inactive governance

1.2 The Solution

Aetheron addresses these pain points through:

  • Polygon Integration: Sub-$0.05 transaction costs and 2-second finality
  • Unified Platform: All DeFi tools in one accessible dashboard
  • Multiple Crypto Payment Options: Pay with BTC, ETH, USDC, and 100+ cryptocurrencies via Coinbase Commerce, BitPay, and NOWPayments
  • High-Yield Staking: Rewards up to 25% APY across multiple pools
  • Social Trading Network: Copy successful traders and share strategies
  • Community Governance: Token-holder voting on all major decisions

5. Governance

5.1 Governance Model

Aetheron implements a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) structure:

  • 1 AETH = 1 Vote: Democratic voting rights
  • Proposal Threshold: 10,000 AETH required to submit proposals
  • Quorum: 10% of circulating supply must participate for validity
  • Voting Period: 7 days per proposal
  • Timelock: 48-hour delay before execution

5.2 Governance Scope

Token holders can vote on:

  • Protocol parameter adjustments (APY rates, lock periods)
  • Treasury fund allocation
  • New feature development priorities
  • Strategic partnerships and integrations
  • Marketing and community initiatives
  • Smart contract upgrades (via proxy patterns)

How It Works

  1. Connect your MetaMask wallet on Polygon network
  2. Enter the amount of AETH and USDC you want to pair
  3. Approve both tokens, then add liquidity in one flow
  4. You'll receive LP tokens representing your pool share
  5. Earn 0.3% fees on every trade in your pool

Important

  • Ensure you're on Polygon Mainnet
  • You need MATIC for gas fees (~0.05 MATIC)
  • Impermanent loss may occur if price changes
  • You can remove liquidity anytime on QuickSwap

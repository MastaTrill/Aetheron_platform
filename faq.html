  4. Earn rewards automatically
  5. Claim rewards or compound anytime
  6. Withdraw after lock period expires

Why is my transaction pending?

Common causes:

  • Low Gas: Increase gas price and retry
  • Network Congestion: Wait a few minutes
  • Nonce Issue: Reset account in MetaMask settings
  • Insufficient Balance: Need MATIC for gas fees

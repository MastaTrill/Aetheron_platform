## ⚠️ Common Rejection Reasons

- Insufficient trading volume or liquidity
- Token launched less than 30 days ago
- Inactive social media accounts
- Broken links in application
- Poor quality or missing logo
- No clear utility or use case
- Suspicious tokenomics (e.g., huge team allocation)
- Unverified smart contract
- Project appears to be a scam

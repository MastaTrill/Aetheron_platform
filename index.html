Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy AETH tokens?

To buy AETH tokens:

  1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask recommended)
  2. Ensure you're on Polygon Mainnet (Chain ID: 137)
  3. Get some MATIC for gas fees
  4. Visit QuickSwap and swap MATIC for AETH

How does staking work?

Staking allows you to earn passive income by locking your AETH tokens for a set period. We offer three pools:

  • 30 Days: 5% APY - Flexible short-term option
  • 90 Days: 12% APY - Medium-term commitment
  • 180 Days: 25% APY - Maximum rewards

Rewards are automatically calculated and can be claimed at any time. Staking is tax-free!

What are the fees?

AETH has a simple tax structure:

  • Buy Tax: 3% (goes to treasury for development)
  • Sell Tax: 5% (supports platform sustainability)
  • Staking: Tax-free transfers
  • Gas Fees: Minimal on Polygon (usually $0.01-0.05)

Is AETH secure and audited?

Yes! Security is our top priority:

  • Built using OpenZeppelin's battle-tested smart contracts
  • All contracts are verified on PolygonScan
  • Liquidity locked on QuickSwap
  • Multi-signature wallet for admin functions
  • Regular security audits and updates

Can I unstake early?

Yes, but with conditions:

  • After Lock Period: Free unstaking with full rewards
  • Before Lock Period: Use "Emergency Withdraw" - you'll get your principal back but forfeit all rewards
  • Recommendation: Plan your staking period carefully to maximize rewards

How do I add AETH to MetaMask?

  • Contract: 0xAb5ae0D8f569d7c2B27574319b864a5bA6F9671e
  • Symbol: AETH
  • Decimals: 18

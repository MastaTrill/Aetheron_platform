# Privacy Policy - Aetheron Mobile App

**Effective Date:** January 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Aetheron ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- Wallet addresses and transaction data
- App usage preferences
- Support requests and communications

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage statistics and crash reports
- Performance metrics

### 2.3 Blockchain Data

- Public blockchain transaction data
- Wallet balances and staking information
- DEX trading history

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Services**: Process transactions, display balances, enable staking
- **Improve App**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, enhance features
- **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements and prevent fraud

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: Analytics and crash reporting services
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
- **Business Transfers**: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure wallet connections
- Regular security audits
- No storage of private keys

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of non-essential communications
- Data portability

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with:

- **WalletConnect**: Secure wallet connections

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Blockchain data is public and permanently recorded.

## 10. International Data Transfers

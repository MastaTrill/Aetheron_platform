## Troubleshooting

### Token doesn't show up
- Make sure you're on **Polygon Mainnet** (not Ethereum or other network)
- Double-check the contract address matches exactly
- Try refreshing MetaMask (Settings â†’ Advanced â†’ Reset Account)

### Shows 0 balance
- The contract may be empty (based on our earlier scan)
- You may need to actually receive or buy AETH tokens first
- Check the contract on PolygonScan to see total supply

### Wrong network
- MetaMask must be on Polygon Mainnet (Chain ID 137)
- The token won't appear on other networks

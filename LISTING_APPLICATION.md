## 7. Tips for Approval (both platforms)

1. **One clean submission beats five partial ones.** Consolidate everything into a single, well-structured application. Piecemeal updates get deprioritized.
2. **Evidence over adjectives.** Every claim (volume, holders, community) should have a verifiable URL or screenshot. Avoid "vibrant", "gaming-grade", "revolutionary".
3. **Match data across sources.** The contract address, symbol, and website must be identical on the website, QuickSwap, Polygonscan, and the application. Incongruities delay verification.
4. **Disclose the tax.** 3%/5% buy/sell tax is fine if disclosed. Hidden taxes cause rejection when reviewers notice mismatched balances.
5. **Use a project-domain email** (e.g. `team@aetheron.io`) rather than a Gmail address — it signals legitimacy. If you only have Gmail, ensure the account is clearly the project's official contact.
6. **Respond to clarification requests within 24 hours.** Both platforms will close stale tickets.
7. **Build community before applying.** Aim for: 100+ Telegram members, 200+ Twitter followers, 100+ unique holders, $50k+ TVL. Below these, approval is still possible but slower.
8. **Do not pay "listing agencies".** Both CoinGecko and CMC explicitly warn against middlemen. Applications are free.
9. **Consider CoinGecko Fast Pass** if you need a guaranteed 24-hour review (paid add-on on the Partners Platform).
10. **Consider CMC Priority (CMCP)** if you need a guaranteed turnaround on CoinMarketCap (official paid service — verify pricing on the CMC help center).

## 8. Known Blockers & Risks for AETH

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **DEX-only listing (QuickSwap)** | Medium | QuickSwap is tracked by both platforms, but DEX-only tokens are benchmarked against other DEX tokens. Ensure TVL and volume are competitive. |
| **Buy/sell tax (3%/5%)** | Medium | Disclose prominently. Some reviewers flag taxed tokens as high-risk. Provide clear documentation of tax purpose. |
| **Circulating supply ambiguity** | High | The 64.05% "Community Reserve" is a large bucket. If most of it is not in circulation, document where it is and whether it is locked. Unverified supply → "−" badge. |
| **Social channels not yet live** | High | Whitepaper lists Twitter, Discord, Telegram as "planned". These MUST be live before applying. |
| **No CEX listing** | Low–Medium | Not required, but a CEX listing (Gate, MEXC, LBank) significantly boosts credibility and ranking. |
| **GitHub Pages domain** | Low | `github.io` is acceptable but a custom domain (e.g. `aetheron.io`) looks more professional. |
| **No external audit yet** | Medium | Whitepaper says audit is "planned Q2 2026". Having an audit (even a basic one) before applying removes a common objection. |
| **Single DEX pair** | Low | Adding a second pair (e.g. AETH/USDC) on QuickSwap or a second DEX improves resilience. |

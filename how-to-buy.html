‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to buy AETH?
You can buy any amount you want! Start with as little as $10-$20. Gas fees on Polygon are very low (usually under $0.01 per transaction).
Is it safe?
The AETH smart contract is deployed on Polygon and is fully auditable. You can view the verified source code on PolygonScan. Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research) before investing.
Can I sell my AETH later?
Yes! Just go back to QuickSwap and swap AETH for POL. You can sell anytime (except for staked tokens that are locked in pools).
What if I accidentally send POL on Ethereum network instead of Polygon?
If you send POL/MATIC on the Ethereum network to a Polygon address, your funds will be stuck. Always double-check you're using the Polygon network! Some exchanges offer recovery services, but it can be expensive.
Do I need to pay taxes on crypto?
In most countries, yes. Crypto transactions are taxable events. Consult with a tax professional in your jurisdiction. Keep records of all your transactions.
